Embrace Honesty With Yourself

Confront your emotions, even if they seem incorrect or frightening. Real change stems from discomfort, by being honest about your feelings. Otherwise, you are just creating a mask that can hinder your ability to live a fulfilling life.

Being honest with yourself does not mean you have to act on every feeling or impulse. It simply means acknowledging and accepting what you genuinely feel, even if it is uncomfortable.
